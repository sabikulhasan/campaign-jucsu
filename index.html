About Me

My name is Zia Uddin Ayan, and I am a proud student of Jahangirnagar University. My journey on this beautiful campus has been one of growth, learning, and forging deep connections within our student community. From my first day here, I've been inspired by the vibrant spirit and intellectual curiosity that defines us.

Through active participation in various student organizations and cultural events, I have gained a firsthand understanding of our collective challenges and aspirations. I am running for JUCSU because I believe in the power of student unity and effective representation. My vision is to build a more inclusive, dynamic, and supportive campus where every student has the opportunity to thrive. I am committed to being your voice, championing our rights, and working tirelessly for a brighter future for all of us at JU.
